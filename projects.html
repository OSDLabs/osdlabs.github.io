  • CS F111 Screencast

    The Computer Programming (CS F111) course is compulsory for all first-year students at BITS Goa. The course has a significant lab component, wherein various documents are provided for students to learn bash, C, and vi.
    OSD Labs is developing screencasts to supplement these documents visually. These are presented as pseudo "videos" with an important caveat - they consist solely of text, which can be copied from the screen and pasted directly onto users machines.

    This project is still in BETA, and will be improved/discontinued based on feedback from first-year students.

    • Bash
    • vi
    • C
    • Asciinema
